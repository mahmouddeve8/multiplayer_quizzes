Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the Multiplayer Quizzes app (the “Application”), created by Mahmoud (“Service Provider”) as a free offline game.

Information Collection and Use

  • The Application does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.
  • No analytics, tracking, or profiling is used.
  • The Application stores only local game data such as player names, scores, and rounds using Shared Preferences. This data remains on your device.

Permissions

  • The Application does not require Internet access for gameplay.
  • Internet may be used only if you choose to share the app via the device’s native share menu.
  • No data is sent to the Service Provider.

Third-Party Services

  • No external analytics or tracking services are used.
  • The Share App feature uses only applications already installed on your device.

Children

  • The Application is suitable for all ages.
  • No personal information is collected from any user.

Changes

Any updates to this Privacy Policy will be posted within the Application.
